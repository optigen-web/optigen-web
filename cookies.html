What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit certain websites. Their main purpose is to recognize your device when you return to the site. Cookies can also be used to improve navigation, personalize the content displayed, and analyze browsing habits.


Types of Cookies Used

This Website may use the following categories of cookies:

 •  Technical or necessary cookies: enable basic website functions and are necessary for the website to operate correctly.

 •  Analytics cookies: allow us or third parties to measure and analyze how users interact with the website.


Consent and Cookie Settings
